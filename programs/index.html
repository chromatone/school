Programs

vmt
Visual Music Theory
Foundational study of Music Theory from a visual standpoint. We revisit all the layers of such vast subject as music as a whole. We'll go through the very basic physics and physiology aspects of seeing and hearing along with the ways to use them as amplifiers for each other to enjoy the world around and inside us.
cjp
Collaborative Jam Performance
Full instructions on how to host and participate in best music improvisation event possible with given sonic space, instruments and participants. Line up, frequency range divisions, harmony planning and timing. Everything you need to prepare and be aware in the process.
lmp
Live Multimedia Production
Very important guidance, that can save thousands of dollars spent in synths that don't work for your creative tasks. We will walk you through all the process from buying to interconnecting all the gear you want to use in your live performances.
cwd
Creative Web-Development
Browser is a universal and very approachable way to make interactive experiences across device designs and generations.
